About Me

Hey, my name's Kenneth. I'm an aspiring games programmer currently studying Computer Games Development at University of the West of Scotland. Over my time at University I've worked on many different types of projects including games, 3D models and animations. I have experience with a variety of different software including Unity, Unreal Engine, Phaser, Autodesk Maya, Blender and Adobe Photoshop.

I have a lot of experience writing programs and scripts in C# and JavaScript and am always keen to improve my knowledge and learn new software. I am currently working on a 3D platformer in Unity Engine, a 2D web game in Phaser and a Virtual Reality game.
