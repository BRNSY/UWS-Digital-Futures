EVENT COUNTDOWN

Date - April 17th 2019 / Room - D133 / Time - 11:00 - 15:00

About Digital Futures


  • Creative Industries showcasing and networking event held at the University of the West of Scotland (Paisley Campus) on the 17th of April 2019.

  • Provides undergraduate students with the opportunity to show case their work to potential employers.

  • Useful B2B networking event for companies in the Creative Industries to meet one another.

  • Allows students to network with people in the Creative Industries community and receive feedback and guidance about their work.

Creative Showcasing Event


  • Undergraduate students from Computer Animation Arts, Computer Games Development and Web and Mobile Development showcasing their portfolios.

  • Students will provide an overview of how they developed their work in addition to show casing their skill sets.

Attending Digital Futures


  • Offers a sound opportunity for potential employers to headhunt talented students and make connections with them.

  • Potential recruiters can meet other individuals from different Creative Industry organisations and backgrounds.

  • Good opportunity to support and enjoy the ongoing creativity and development in an industry and that is continuously evolving and expanding.

  • Opportunity to know more about the Creative Industry programmes run at UWS
